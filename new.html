chocolate chip cookie recipe

ingredients

  • 1 cup butter or margarine,softened
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 eggs, large
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 1/4 cups flour,unsifted
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup walnuts or pecans, chopped(optional)
  • 2 cups KCC bittersweet 60% cacao Baking chips
    •

    Baking Instruction

  • 1. Heat oven to 375 fahranheit
  • 2. Stir flour with baking soda and saly; set aside
  • 3. In large mixing bowl.beat butter with sugar ,and brown sugar at medium speed until creamy and lightened in color
  • 4. Add eggs and vanilla ,one at a time .mix on low speed until incorporated.
  • 5. Gradually blend dry mixturw into creamed mixture .stir in nuts and chocolart chips.
  • 6. Drop by tablespoon onto ungreased cookie sheets
  • 7. Bake for 9 to 11 minute or until chocolate chip cookies are golden brown
  • 8. Enjoy
